Free · Open source · Windows 10/11

A small, quick Windows app for ffmpeg.

Drag in a video — crop, trim, resize, transform, adjust, overlay, and convert it. Then export a clip that plays everywhere.

One window. The whole edit.

No timeline soup, no sign-in, no upload. Open a file, make your cut, and export — Leike drives ffmpeg under the hood and gets out of your way.

Everything you need

Packed, not bloated.

Crop with presets

Drag a box on the preview. Lock it to 9:16, 1:1, 4:5, 16:9 — or go free.

Trim & filmstrip

Scrub a live preview with a thumbnail strip. Lossless fast-trim when the cut allows.

MP4 · WebM · GIF · MP3

Export H.264 by default, or switch format in a click — including audio-only.

GPU encoding

NVENC acceleration kicks in automatically when a compatible GPU is present.

Transforms

Rotate, mirror, change speed, fade, aspect-fill, reverse, boomerang, loop.

Color & cleanup

Brightness, contrast, saturation, grayscale, denoise, sharpen.

Overlays

Burn in a text caption, an image watermark, or a subtitle file.

Fit a size limit

Target a max file size and Leike picks the bitrate with a two-pass encode.

Stabilization

Smooth shaky footage with a two-pass deshake.

Grab a frame

Save the current preview frame as a still image, instantly.

Audio control

Mute, set the volume, or rip the audio out to MP3.

Fully local

Nothing uploads. Your files never leave your machine. Settings are remembered.
